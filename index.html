Biography

Shaobo Cui now works as an algorithm engineer in DAMO Academy, Alibaba. Before that he obtained MSc in Computer Science from Tsinghua University(2019) and B.E and B.A from Beihang University(2016). His research interests include machine learning, natural language processing.
